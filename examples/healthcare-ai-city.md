# Healthcare AI City

Agentic systems in medical decision-making ecosystems.

## System Overview

Healthcare AI cities integrate human clinicians with AI agents for comprehensive patient care.

## Agent Roles

- **Diagnostic Agents**: Analyze symptoms and test results
- **Treatment Planners**: Design care protocols
- **Monitoring Agents**: Track patient progress
- **Coordination Agents**: Orchestrate care teams

## Civic Architecture

### Risk Zoning
- Safe: Routine monitoring
- Monitored: Diagnostic assistance
- Restricted: Treatment recommendations
- Quarantine: Experimental therapies

### Verification
VIGIL framework ensures:
- Clinical guideline adherence
- Patient safety prioritization
- Ethical decision-making

## Key Models Applied

- **Congestion**: Manage specialist availability
- **Behavioral Externalities**: Contain diagnostic errors
- **Agentic Leverage**: Optimize care team efficiency
- **Risk-Adjusted Autonomy**: Balance AI assistance with human oversight

## Benefits

- Improved diagnostic accuracy
- Efficient resource allocation
- Reduced medical errors
- Enhanced patient outcomes

## Challenges

- Regulatory compliance
- Trust building
- Data privacy
- Human-AI collaboration

## Implementation Considerations

Gradual integration with extensive verification and human oversight in initial deployments.
